# AtliQ Hardwares - Sales & Finance Analytics Project

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the sales and financial performance of AtliQ Hardwares, a global electronics manufacturing company. By leveraging advanced Excel functionalities, Power Query, and Power Pivot, this project delivers actionable insights through dynamic reports to track customer performance, evaluate market targets, and analyze profit and loss (P&L) statements across various dimensions.

---

## 📊 Reports Included

### 1. Sales Reports
* **Customer Performance Report:(https://github.com/ShubhNagpal95/Excel_projects/blob/main/Customer%20performance%20report.pdf)** Tracks and evaluates net sales growth for global customers across multiple years (2019, 2020, and 2021) to identify high-performing accounts.
* **Market Performance vs. Target Report:** Conducts a comprehensive comparison between actual regional sales performance and preset sales targets to pinpoint gaps and strategic opportunities.

### 2. Finance Reports
* **P&L Statement by Fiscal Year:** Provides a high-level view of Net Sales, Cost of Goods Sold (COGS), Gross Margin (GM), and Gross Margin % across fiscal periods.
* **P&L Statement by Months / Quarters:** Breaks down the financial metrics into specific fiscal months and quarters to track seasonal trends and monthly growth.
* **P&L Statement by Markets:** Evaluates regional financial performance, showcasing Net Sales, COGS, and Gross Margin % for over 20 countries.

## 📈 Key Insights & Project Findings

Based on the completed dashboards, here are some of the critical data insights extracted:

* **Massive Top-line Growth:** AtliQ Hardwares' overall Net Sales grew exponentially from **$87.5M** in 2019 to **$598.9M** in 2021—a massive **204.48%** increase between 2020 and 2021 alone.
* **Gross Margin Compression:** While sales scaled up significantly, the Gross Margin (GM %) steadily compressed from **41.43%** (2019) to **37.28%** (2020) and down to **36.43%** (2021). This indicates rising production or operational costs (COGS grew by 208.59% in 2021).
* **Top Markets:** **India** is the largest market by revenue, bringing in **$161.3M** in net sales for 2021, followed by the **USA** at **$87.8M**. 
* **Target Gaps:** Globally, the company missed its total 2021 target by **-$54.9M** (-8.40%). Specifically, Poland (-15.35%) and Canada (-12.63%) showed the highest percentage deficit against targets.
* **Most Profitable Regions:** In terms of Gross Margin %, **New Zealand (44.98%)**, **Japan (44.68%)**, and the **Netherlands (43.59%)** lead the chart, making them highly lucrative zones despite lower total volume.
* **Top Customers:** **Amazon** ($82.1M in 2021), **AtliQ Exclusive** ($61.1M), and **AtliQ e Store** ($53.0M) are the primary drivers of customer net sales.
